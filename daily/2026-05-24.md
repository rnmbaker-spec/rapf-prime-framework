## 1) Mertens Product Framework

### Mertens' Theorem
For large $ X $, the product over odd primes $ p ≤ X $ exhibits asymptotic behavior:
$$
\prod_{3 ≤ p ≤ X} − \left(1 - \frac{1}{p}\right) = \frac{e^{-\gamma}}{\log X} \cdot \left(1 + o(1)\right)
$$
where $ \gamma $ is Euler-Mascheroni constant (~0.5772).

### Polynomial-GCD Bridge
The framework's polynomial-GCD score mechanics relate to the reciprocal:
$$
