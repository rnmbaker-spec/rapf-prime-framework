## Key Findings

### Proof by Contradiction Architecture
Developed a formal contradiction proof draft:
1. Assume twin primes are finite with largest pair (N, N+2)
2. Then π₂(X) = constant for X ≥ N
3. But v4.3 shows λ̂/λ_HL converges monotonically through 4 decades:
   0.8470 → 0.8617 → 0.8837 → 0.8978
4. Under finite assumption, this ratio must flatten to constant C₂/π₂(N)
5. No mechanism exists for the observed convergence to suddenly stop
6. Contradiction → twin primes are infinite

### Three Independent Contradiction Lines
1. **Convergence structure** (strongest): Ratio improvement incompatible with
   finite twins. Detailed in Section 3.1 of draft.
2. **Fisher information singularity**: I(λ) requires infinite sample. Draft
   in Section 3.2 — needs formalization.
3. **Admissible class occupancy forcing**: A_n always grows; finite twins
   would leave infinitely many classes permanently empty. Draft in 3.3.

## Open Questions

1. **The "Eventually" gap**: Ratio increases through 10⁹ but could theoretically
   flatten at some N > 10⁹. Need a rate-of-convergence theorem to close this.
2. **Fisher information formalization**: Need rigorous proof that I(λ) > 0
   requires infinitely many twin primes.
3. **Admissible class forcing lemma**: If A_n classes exist with CV → 0, prove
   at least one must contain a twin prime.
4. **Spectral bridge**: 68% orbital overlap stability is empirical; needs to
   become a formal lemma.

## Peer Review Feedback (Gemini)

Excellent adversarial review identified three critical issues:

1. **Circularity trap (Section 4)**: Using Fisher Information derived under HL
   assumptions to prove HL must hold is circular. If twins are finite, the
   model is simply wrong — I(λ) → 0 is natural, not a contradiction.

2. **Inductive leap (Section 5)**: The O(√Li₂(X)) error bound is RH-level
   strength. v4.3 showed Cramér-Rao lower bounds on σ, not upper bounds on E(X).
   Cannot deduce |E(X)| ≤ √(A_n · Li₂(X)) from CV → 0 alone.

3. **Recommended pivot**: Reframe from "proof" to "heuristic boundary analysis"
   — quantify the structural shock required by finite scenario, don't claim
   it proves infinitude.
