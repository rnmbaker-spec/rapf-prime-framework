## Opus Critiques → Fixes

### 1. FUSCHIAN TOPOLOGICAL MISMATCH ✓ FIXED → Bogomolny–Keating Universality
**Opus caught:** The cylinder $\mathbb{R}^+ \times S^1$ is not isometric to
the modular surface $\Gamma_0(210) \backslash \mathbb{H}^2$. The Fuchsian
quotient claim was topologically wrong. Mapping prime gaps to closed geodesics
in the quotient requires a massive algebraic leap (Pell's equation solutions)
that we bypassed.

**Fix:** Section 5 rewritten. Dropped $\Gamma_0(210)$ isometry entirely.
Now uses **Bogomolny–Keating heuristic** + **Gutzwiller trace formula** to
connect hyperbolic gap dynamics to GUE universality class. The connection is
via spectral statistics universality, not geometric isometry. Much more honest
and mathematically defensible. Same logarithmic density $\rho(\gamma) \sim
\frac{1}{2\pi}\log(\gamma/2\pi)$, same Montgomery pair correlation, but
now via the correct mathematical pathway.

### 2. SIEGEL-WALFISZ CATEGORY ERROR ✓ FIXED → Gallagher's Theorem
**Opus caught:** S-W bounds prime *counts* in residue classes, not the
parametric density *shape* of gaps. Equidistribution ≠ exponential shape
preservation. Fisher conditioning $p(x|\lambda,\mathcal{A}) = p(x|\lambda)/C_2$
was not rigorously justified.

**Fix:** Section 6 rewritten. Replaced S-W with **Gallagher's theorem (1976)**:
under Hardy–Littlewood $k$-tuple conjectures, primes in short intervals follow
a Poisson distribution. Poisson → inter-arrival times (gaps) are exponential
$p(x|\lambda) = \lambda^{-1} e^{-x/\lambda}$. The singular series acts as
multiplicative constant $C_2$, leaving parametric shape invariant. Fisher
scaling $1/C_2$ is now rigorously anchored in established analytic number theory.

### 3. MAP-VS-TERRITORY CONFLATION ✓ FIXED → Information-Theoretic Obstructions
**Opus caught:** The model's non-vanishing metric doesn't force the discrete
primes to continue. A smooth manifold can have emergent thresholds/phase
transitions without singularities. The "proof by contradiction" conflated
theoretical model smoothness with number-theoretic reality.

**Fix:** Section 7 completely reframed:
- **Retitled:** "Information-Theoretic Obstructions to Finiteness" (no longer claims proof)
- **Step 4 rewritten as "Map-vs-Territory Obstruction":** Shows that finite twins
  would create irreconcilable tension between 4 independent pillars (geometry,
  modular arithmetic, Fisher metric, computational evidence). Not a formal
  contradiction, but a *geometric obstruction*: the burden of proof shifts to
  identifying a decoupling mechanism. Academic honesty preserved.
